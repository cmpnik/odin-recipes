Spaghetti

Description

Step into the heart of Italian culinary tradition with our Timeless Spaghetti Elegance - a celebration of simplicity, flavor, and the comforting allure of a classic pasta dish. This recipe captures the essence of homemade goodness, featuring al dente spaghetti tossed in a luscious tomato sauce infused with garlic, basil, and aromatic herbs. Each twirl of spaghetti promises a delightful harmony of textures and a burst of rich, savory flavors that transport you to the rustic kitchens of Italy.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (about 225g) spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the Spaghetti:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
    • Pour in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, dried basil, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if using.
    • Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, allowing it to thicken and the flavors to meld.
  3. Combine Pasta and Sauce:
    • Add the cooked and drained spaghetti to the skillet with the tomato sauce.
    • Toss the spaghetti in the sauce, ensuring each strand is well-coated. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a bit of the reserved pasta water to reach your desired consistency.
  4. Serve:
    • Divide the spaghetti among plates.
    • Garnish with torn fresh basil leaves.
    • Optionally, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Enjoy:
    • Serve immediately, and savor the simple yet delightful flavors of this classic pasta dish.
